# AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses

## 📌 Project Overview

This project uses AI (ChatGPT) to generate complete website content for local businesses like salons, clinics, and gyms.

---

## 🧠 Prompt Used

Act as a professional website copywriter and marketing expert.

Create a complete website copy for a local business.

Business Details:

* Business Name: Niharika Boutiques & Parlour
* Location: Hyderabad
* Target Audience: Women
* Services: Parlour services
* USP: Affordable and stylish beauty services

---

## 📥 Input Example

Niharika Boutiques & Parlour in Hyderabad targeting women customers.

---

## 📤 Output

### Homepage

Enhance Your Beauty with Style & Elegance
At Niharika Boutiques & Parlour, Hyderabad, we bring out your natural beauty.

---

### Services

* Facial & Skin Care
* Hair Care & Styling
* Bridal Makeup
* Manicure & Pedicure
* Boutique Services

---

### Why Choose Us

* Experienced professionals
* Affordable pricing
* Customer satisfaction

---

### Call To Action

Book your appointment today!

This project demonstrates how AI can generate website content using prompt engineering.
